eNcade Redesign

 
The Raspberry Pi 2 has been released, and the eNcade will be utilizing it in our latest redesign. Along with design improvements, we have made the latest model more modular, allowing existing Pi owners to purchase just the shell, with our proprietary PCB built in, of the eNcade or with accessories. Of course customers will still be able to purchase the eNcade pre-assembled with a Raspberry Pi. Check out this page to see a full list of the new features!
