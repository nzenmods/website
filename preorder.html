eNcade pre-orders are now being taken

Pre-order yours today out of these options:

  • eNcade Basic: If you already own a Raspberry Pi 2 or B+, this is the option for you. What you will receive is an assembled eNcade with integrated LCD display, gaming controls, 2500 mAh battery, charger, and of course our proprietary PCB technology. All you need to do is plug in your own Pi and go. 
    • *This option does not include an SD card or Wi-Fi dongle, we will release a free download of our software once these units are shipped that you will be able to load on your own micro SD card (4GB minimum) at home.

  • eNcade Core: The essentials. What you will receive is the eNcade Basic package with the addition of an included Raspberry Pi 2. All you need to do is load our software on your own micro SD card and you're ready to go!
    • *In order to play online, you will need to provide your own USB Wi-Fi dongle.

  • eNcade Standard: If you want a complete game console out of the box, this is the option for you. You will receive the eNcade Basic package with assembled Raspberry Pi 2, pre-loaded micro SD card, and Wi-Fi dongle. Everything you need to start retro gaming with your buddies across the world (and possibly the Universe).
