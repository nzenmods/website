The eNcade





 

The eNcade is a new portable game console based on the Raspberry Pi 2. Utilizing emulation and the flexibility of the Raspberry Pi, players will now have the ability to access retro games on a variety of consoles with online multiplayer.


 
Why eNcade?


 
Our focus is to bring modern game elements to retro game emulation. We understand and acknowledge other game consoles that focus on emulation that are currently on the market. Our intent however, is to bring new features, like online multiplayer and voice chat, through our software frontend. With the combined use of Raspberry Pi hardware, the eNcade is truly a modern open source approach to playing the classics you love.
 

















 
Redesign




 
Changelog
 
Completely new case design. The new design allows for the user to easily remove/replace every aspect of the device. The new case is more ergonomic and aesthetic than all previous designs.

Less expensive. To go along with the new case, we have been looking at ways to make the eNcade more affordable. We have decided that we want to appeal to the existing Raspberry Pi community, and as such the eNcade is now modular enough to allow existing Raspberry Pi owners to simply plug their device into the eNcade casing, from the GPIO header on the eNcade's included proprietary PCB, and use its controls and screen. Now you can simply detach your Pi from the eNcade's PCB header and use it with your TV whenever you want, making the eNcade a plug-and-play device out of the box.

Accessories. This new concept allows us to sell everything related to the eNcade however the user wants. Already have a WiFi adapter or plan on using ethernet? Simply remove the parts from your order when purchasing the device. This puts the customer first and foremost, giving control to the user instead of greedy companies.
